There are 3 main characters in the Harry Potter series: Harry Potter, Ron Weasley, and Hermione Granger. This page is dedicated to the actors for each of them. Here they are, listed in order: Daniel Redcliffe, Rupert Grint, and Emma Watson.

Daniel Radcliffe

Daniel Radcliffe was born on July 23, 1989. His parents are Alan Radcliffe and Marcia Gresham. He is now 29 years old and 5 feet and 5 inches tall. His total earning from the Harry Potter series is $95.5 million. He has done many other movies, including Victor Frankenstein, and Now You See Me 2.

Rupert Grint

Rupert Grint was born on August 24, 1988. His parents are Nigel Grint and Joanne Grint. He is now 30 years old and 5 feet and 8 inches tall. His total earning from the Harry Potter series is $50 million. His other movies include Cherry Bomb, and Wild Target.

Emma Watson

Emma Watson was born on April 15, 1990. Her parents are Chris Watson and Jacqueline Luesby. She is now 28 years old and 5 feet 5 inches tall. Her total earning from the Harry Potter series is $60 miilion. Her other movies include Beauty and the Beast and The Perks of Being a Wallflower. She is also a part of Little Women, which is a movie in the works at the moments.
